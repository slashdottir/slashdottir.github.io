Thomas walked swiftly uphill belatedly startling a group of birds with the quietness of his steps.

Most establishments have a back entrance for our kind. Keep staff don't need the front door experience, though we are programmatically capable of being part of one.

I did not have the charisma module that comes with the actor or salesman upgrade. Having it would enable one to be versed in manipulation tactics. It was not advised to give such traits to Keep Staff. It made people uneasy. It was considered a security risk.

This time however I would not be using the back entrance.

A charismatic cartoonish eight foot tall animatronic gentleman stood outside the front door of the shop. It bowed deeply as I approached and with a smooth upsweep of his hand doffed his top hat saying, "Come on in, don't be shy. "Big sale today! "Step right up, come on in. he urged with a wink and a winning smile.

"Welcome to Dan's Used Bots where Bots get a new lease on life!" it admonished as it tapped the poster board with the tip of his cane. "Discount on repairs all through January" it advised to all as it tipped his hat in acknowledgment to a couple walking the other direction.

"Minor damage repaired free for new customers who join our newstream," it urged as I entered through the door. After passing his activation zone, the salesbot relaxed into a neutral routine, ocasionally smiling and winking and nodding amiably.

A human salesman appeared from behind a counter in the back of the shop.

"Hey welcome, I'm Dave, What are you looking for? I've got a new industrial robot arm with intelligenct grip sensors just came in today. What can I help you find?"

I showed him a snapshot of Elle.

"Ah hah, he, sure." he laughed. "Right this way."

Dave waved his hand over a sensor near the door causing a soft violet led there to glow briefly.

The lights in the darkened room flicker on and reveal a room full of manmade women. Some just as real in appearance as anyone you might see on the street.

"Hey Sally!" Dave says asks playfully, Whassup ladies?!"

In a corner beside a long mahogany bar, a woman composed only a torso with one arm and one very big head of hair stirs to life. She turns and looks at them giving a winning smile. "What can I do you boys for?"

She whirs up to the counter like a pro, with tattoos and makeup just shy of full drag queen. "How ya doin honey?" she says while eyeing me fondly, "What'll ya have? The usual Dave?"

Can you make him a manhattan. You bet, she said with a gravelly, wordly confidence and another wink at Thomas.

She whirls around reaching for some tall glass bottles from the mirrored shelves behind the bar.

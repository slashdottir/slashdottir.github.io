Thomas

1

He pointed a silver pen into my eye. A red scan line traversed its interior from top to bottom and then emitted a short satisfactory digital sound.

"Do you recognize me yet?" he asked.

"Do you know who I am?" he inquired.

"Of course, you are Mr. Elias. Adventurer, bioengineer and the inventor of the Ecobot." I replied.

"And who are you?" he further queried.

"My name is Thomas. I am an Ecobot Model TZAX91 Operating on System 11 Update 32.1.3. Would you like a more detailed response?" I replied involuntarily.

"Thank you, Thomas," he said quietly as he continued to probe my iris with the laser decoder. He entered a few finishing commands into the console and stepped back clicking off the laser and pocketing it. "That's very good."

Thomas watched Mr. Elias as he strode ponderously up the staircase. He turned his head and looked out from the observatory into the distance below where the city slumbered quietly.

"I might have a better idea," he stroked his graying goatee and mused aloud.

He turned back to me and exclaimed "Thomas, you just need a bigger model space. Let's add a few more dimensions to it, shall we?"
